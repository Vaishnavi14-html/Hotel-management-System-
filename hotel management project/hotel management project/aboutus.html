ABOUT HOTEL LANDMARK

To become the most trusted and beloved destination in Gadchiroli by offering heartfelt hospitality, refined comfort, and authentic local experiences — where every guest feels at home, every stay tells a story, and every moment becomes a memory.

Our Vision

Design-led and story-inspired, our guest rooms are as comfortable as they are beautiful. Sink into the plush bedding, admire the handcrafted details and enjoy elevated touches that restore and recharge.

Our Mission

Our spa-inspired bathrooms make it all about you – whether you're kicking off your day or winding it down. Primp, preen, and indulge with glass-enclosed walk-in showers and beautifully lit mirrors.

OUR TEAM

At Hotel Landmark, it’s the people who make the place truly special. From the warm smiles at the door to the meticulous care behind every detail, our team is the heart of our hospitality. Each member brings passion, experience, and a drive to create memorable moments for every guest.
